About Us!

Welcome to Paw Trails! We are Jacob and Allison Stevens and have a passion for adventure. Specifically, outdoor adventure with our two dogs, Maya and Cairo. We made this small hiking blog to help others that also have a passion for adventure and love doing it with the whole family! (Fur buddies included!) We investigate hiking trails around the Salt Lake City area and the state of Utah that allow our furry friends to enjoy the outdoors just as much as we do.
